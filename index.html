How to Play - Fruit Box

  • Choose 2, 3, or 4 numbers that add up to 10 (examples: 7+2+1, 4+3+2+1, 5+5, etc.)
  • Available numbers are 1-9 only
  • If the combination is incorrect, he selected numbers will be reset and 10 points will be deducted
  • Each correct combination will award points based on the sum of the digits: 10 points for 2 numbers, 15 for 3 numbers, 20 for 4 numbers
  • The system will always ensure there are possible combinations available
  • Use the "Get Hints" button if you are having trouble finding a combination
  • Be careful with mistakes! Too many mistakes will reduce your score.
